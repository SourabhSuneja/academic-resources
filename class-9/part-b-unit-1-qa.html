Introduction to Data - Q&A

Short Answer Type Questions

1. Write any four qualities of information.

Four qualities of information are: Accuracy, Relevance, Timeliness, and Completeness.

2. What is the objective of Information Technology (IT)?

The main goal of IT is to store, retrieve, process, and share data efficiently using computers and networks.

3. What is the meaning of knowledge in DIKW model?

Knowledge means understanding and using the correct information to make smart decisions.

4. What is the impact of data in healthcare industry?

Data helps in diagnosing diseases, tracking health records, and giving better treatment to patients.

5. Who might track your digital footprints?

Digital footprints may be tracked by advertisers, websites, social media apps, and even hackers.

Long Answer Type Questions

1. Give some examples of how data impacts our daily life.

  • Weather apps: Use data to show temperature and forecasts.
  • Maps: Use traffic and location data to give best routes.
  • Shopping websites: Suggest items based on our past data.
  • Fitness apps: Track steps, sleep, and calories using our data.

2. Explain the reasons for different types of digital data loss.

  • Hardware failure: Broken hard drives or devices.
  • Viruses: Harmful software that damages files.
  • Accidental deletion: Removing files by mistake.
  • Theft or hacking: When someone steals or locks your data.

3. How data footprints affect you? What are the different types of data footprints? Explain in detail.

Data footprints can affect privacy and safety. They can be used to show ads, or in some cases, harm your reputation.

  • Active footprints: Data you share by posting or filling forms online.
  • Passive footprints: Data collected without you knowing, like your browsing habits.

4. How do you achieve the required goals through DIKW model?

We start with data, organize it to form information, use it to build knowledge, and apply that knowledge wisely to gain wisdom. This step-by-step use helps us reach our goals.

5. Why is a data recovery plan necessary?

A data recovery plan helps you get back lost files during emergencies. It protects important data from loss due to accidents, viruses, or system failures.

6. How do online streaming platforms use data?

Streaming platforms collect data like what you watch, when you watch, and for how long. They use this data to give personalized recommendations and improve your viewing experience.
